A new type of ransomware has been discovered recently. This ransomware, known as fsociety ransomware, is a variant of EDA2, and what makes it special is that it is named after the hacker group in the TV fiction series Mr. Robot. In the series, fsociety is a hacking group that tries to bring down E Corp, a financial conglomerate, with the purpose of altering the current world order. In one of the attacks against E Corp, fsociety uses a ransomware that resembles the real malware CryptoWall, but displaying a wallpaper with an image of the infamous mask used by the group. #MrRobot
